Welcome!

Make Push-To-Talk Calls Across the Country with iDEN! iDEN is a smartphone Push-To-Talk 2-way walkie-talkie app that lets users communicate with individuals or a group via Push to Talk (PTT) wherever Wi-Fi®, 4G LTE, 4G, or 3G service is available. iDEN communicates with any smartphone that has iDEN installed and activated. Making and answering a iDEN calls is easy: To make a call, just select a person or a talk group from your contacts list and press the Bixby® button to begin a call. With iDEN,, you have the option of sending an Alert, turning off your phone’s speaker for a private conversation, or pressing your designated Bixby® button to begin a call.
